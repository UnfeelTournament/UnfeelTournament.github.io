To download the game:

Go to: https://github.com/UnfeelTournament/UnfeelTournament

  1. Click "Clone or download"
  2. Click "Download ZIP"
  3. Unzip the "Unfeel-Tournament-master" folder
  4. Click the "Unfeel-Tournament-master" folder
  5. Click the "UnfeelTournament" folder
  6. Unzip the "Build.zip" folder
  7. Open the "Build.zip" folder
  8. Click "UFT-Build v1.0.exe"
  9. Play the game with the screen as 1280 x 720"
  10. Enjoy!

Narrative

  • May 27th, 2016

    The Walk Home

    Billy was walking home from school when he learned about the news of Harambe’s death. Should he feel sad about the gorilla’s death? Should he be happy that the child was alive? Or angry that this was somehow the child's parent's fault? Should he feel worried that a rare gorilla was killed?

  • He couldn't help but to think about what was happening...

  • Inside his head, Happy, Sad, Worried, and Angry were aware of the situation. They knew Billy was confused and that he was trying to figure out how to feel. Each emotion knew that this was a chance to take over Billy's brain and control the situation.

  • Each with their own objective and determined to take over Billy's brain, the emotions escaped their cells ready to battle each other. Who will win?
